❌ Beginner Mistakes

❌ 1. High Sensitivity

Beginners often use very high sensitivity which reduces aim control and accuracy.

❌ 2. No Warm-up

Jumping directly into ranked games without practice leads to poor performance.

❌ 3. Bad Positioning

Standing in open areas makes you an easy target for enemies.

❌ 4. No Game Sense

Not understanding maps and enemy positions leads to unnecessary deaths.
