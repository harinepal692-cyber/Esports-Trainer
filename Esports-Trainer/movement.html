🏃 Complete Movement Guide

🏃 1. Strafing

Move left and right while shooting to make yourself harder to hit. Never stand still in fights.

🧱 2. Cover Usage

Always use walls, rocks, and objects as cover. Open area fights should be avoided unless necessary.

🎯 3. Peek Timing

Peek corners quickly and return back to cover. Don’t stay exposed for too long.

⚡ 4. Advanced Movement

Learn jump peek, crouch peek, and zig-zag movement to confuse enemies.
