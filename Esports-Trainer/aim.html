🎯 Complete Aim Training Guide

Learn how to improve your aim step by step

🎯 1. Sensitivity Setup

The most important part of aim improvement is sensitivity. Use low or medium sensitivity so you can control recoil better. High sensitivity makes your aim unstable and inconsistent.

🖱️ 2. Crosshair Placement

Always keep your crosshair at head level. This reduces reaction time and helps you get instant kills. Never aim at the ground while moving.

🎮 3. Daily Practice

Practice 15–30 minutes daily in training mode or aim trainers. Consistency is more important than long sessions.

🔥 4. Spray Control

Learn recoil patterns of your favorite weapons. Practice pulling your aim down slowly while shooting.
